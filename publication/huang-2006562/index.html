Melting pressure equation for helium-3

Y.-H. Huang, G.-B. Chen
January 2006
Cite URL

Abstract

Based on a thorough survey of experimental data and theoretical correlations, a melting pressure equation for 3He valid in a wide range was developed. This nonsegment equation, in SI unit, precisely describes the pressure and temperature relation along the melting curve of 3He at temperatures from 0.001 K to 30 K. The maximum and the average differences between calculations by the melting pressure equation and 406 measurements are 3.02% and 0.106%, respectively. A minimum pressure 2.93113 MPa is predicted at 0.31586 K, which agrees with early experimental and theoretical studies fairly well.
